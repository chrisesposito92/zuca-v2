# Create Roku Products in the Zuora Product Catalog

Synchronize Roku products with Zuora to improve visibility and reporting by adding and configuring products in the Zuora Product Catalog.

Maintain synchronization between Roku products and Zuora product listings to enhance visibility and reporting. :

1.  Navigate to the Zuora Dashboard .
2.  Go to the Product Catalog and select Add Product .
3.  Enter the necessary details for Roku products, including names, descriptions, and pricing.
4.  Save the new products to the catalog.
