# Enable History Tracking for the Account Field

Learn how to enable history tracking for the Account field in the Billing Account object.

To enable History Tracking for the Account field of the Billing Account object:

1.  Navigate to Setup > Build > Create > Objects .
2.  Click Billing Account .
3.  In the Custom Fields and Relationships section, click Set History Tracking .
4.  On the Billing Account Field History page, select Account .
5.  Click Save .
