# Launch NetSuite ERP connector

Learn how to launch the Revenue Inbound connector for NetSuite ERP.

1.  Navigate to from your Zuora UI.

    If you have Zuora Billing to Zuora Revenue OTR integration, you can access from either the Billing tenant or Revenue tenant. Revenue-only users can access from the Revenue tenant.

2.  Select the connector with the name NetSuite ERP.

Enter authentication details for NetSuite and Zuora Revenue in the connector. See [Authentication credentials for NetSuite and Zuora Revenue](/zuora-platform/integration/integration-hub/revenue-connector-for-netsuite-erp/configurations-in-connector/launch-netsuite-erp-connector/authentication-credentials-for-netsuite-and-zuora-revenue).

As required, you can use the integration toggle to activate or deactivate the connector.
