# Configure email notifications for GL connector

Learn how to configure email notifications for SAP GL (Revenue) in the Integration Hub.

1.  Navigate to Marketplace > Integration Hub .
2.  Search and select SAP GL (Revenue) .
3.  Click Authentication .
4.  Click the Email icon.
5.  Turn on the Email settings toggle.
6.  Select Failure and Success (optional).
7.  Click Add Email to add recipients.
8.  Click Save .
