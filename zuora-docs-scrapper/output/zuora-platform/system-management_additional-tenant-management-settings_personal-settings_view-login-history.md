# View login history

Learn how to view your login history, including login date, status, and browser information.

1.  Click your avatar in the upper right of the Zuora UI and then click Profile. The Personal Settings page opens.
2.  Click Manage Your Profile.
3.  Scroll to the bottom and click Show Login History.
4.  View the following login information in the Login History table:

    -   Date: The date of the login attempt.

    -   Status: Whether the login attempt was a `Success` or `Failure`.

    -   Type: The type of browser that you used to log in to Zuora. For example, FireFox or Internet Explorer.

    -   Version: The version of the browser that you used.
