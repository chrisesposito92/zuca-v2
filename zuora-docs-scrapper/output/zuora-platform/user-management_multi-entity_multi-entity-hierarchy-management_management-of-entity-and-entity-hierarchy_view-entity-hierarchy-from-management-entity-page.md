# View entity hierarchy from management entity page

Learn how to view the entity hierarchy from the management entity page.

1.  Log in as an administrator of the global entity.
2.  Navigate to the global entity from the [entity switcher](/zuora-platform/user-management/multi-entity/overview-of-multi-entity).
3.  Click your username at the top right and navigate to Administration > Manage Entity Hierarchy .

You can view the entity hierarchy with all the provisioned and unprovisioned entities on the Manage Hierarchy of Entities page.
