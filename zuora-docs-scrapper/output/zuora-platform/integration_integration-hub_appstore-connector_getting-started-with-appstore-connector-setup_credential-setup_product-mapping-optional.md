# Product Mapping (Optional)

Map Roku product identifiers to Zuora product rate plans to ensure accurate subscription data reflection.

Ensure accurate reflection of subscription data from Roku Pay by mapping Roku product identifiers to Zuora product rate plans. :

1.  Go to the Product Catalog in Zuora.
2.  Select Rate Plans and choose the rate plans to map.
3.  Enter the Roku product identifier (e.g., channel\_id ) in the corresponding fields and add it to the ExternallyManagedPlanID field as needed.
4.  Save the mappings to link Roku products with Zuora rate plans.
