# Create Google Products in the Zuora Product Catalog (Optional)

Create Google Play products in the Zuora Product Catalog by navigating to the dashboard, adding products, entering details, and saving them.

Align Zuora product listings with Google Play products to improve management and reporting. This step is optional but helpful for maintaining synchronized and accurate records. :

1.  Navigate to the Zuora Dashboard .
2.  Go to the Product Catalog and select Add Product .
3.  Enter details for Google Play products, including names, descriptions, and pricing.
4.  Save the new products to the catalog.
