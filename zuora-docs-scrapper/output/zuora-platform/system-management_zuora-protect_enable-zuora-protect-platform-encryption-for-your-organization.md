# Enable Zuora Protect platform encryption for your organization

Learn how to enable Zuora Protect platform encryption by contacting Zuora Support and setting up encryption keys.

To enable your organization's Zuora Protect Platform Encryption feature, contact Zuora Support at [support@zuora.com](mailto:support@zuora.com) . Our team will help you set up the feature. Once your account is ready, you can create the encryption keys and encrypt data.

## Prerequisites

We recommend you familiarize yourself with the Zuora objects and fields that support BYOK encryption. For more information, see[Support Fields for BYOK Encryption](/zuora-platform/system-management/zuora-protect/enable-zuora-protect-platform-encryption-for-your-organization/support-fields-for-byok-encryption).
