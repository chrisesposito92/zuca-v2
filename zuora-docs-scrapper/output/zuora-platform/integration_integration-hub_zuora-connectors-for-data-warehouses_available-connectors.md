# Available connectors

This reference provides information on the connectors that Zuora provides.

Note:

The Zuora Connector for Data Warehouses feature is in the Early availability phase. This is a paid add-on. If you are interested in joining our early availability program, please reach out to your Zuora Representative for further details.

Zuora offers a range of connectors to suit your organization's data storage and analysis needs. Each connector is specifically designed for optimal performance with its corresponding data destination, ensuring that you can leverage the full power of your data warehouse or storage solution.

Zuora provides the connectors listed in the following table. Refer to the individual connector page to learn more about setting up and getting started with a connector.

Connectors marked with Asterisks (\*\*) are available as part of the Early Availability program with 24-hour refresh rate support .

| Data Warehouse | Zuora Connector for AWS Redshift **Zuora Connector for BigQuery **Zuora Connector for Databricks ** |
