# Overview of Zuora Connector for BigQuery

Directly access your Zuora data through BigQuery

Note:

The Zuora Connector for Data Warehouses feature is in the Early availability phase. This is a paid add-on. If you are interested in joining our early availability program, please reach out to your Zuora Representative for further details.

Enable your organization to directly access your Zuora data through BigQuery. This integration involves a straightforward one-time setup process, which removes the usual technical burdens associated with managing data extracts, integrations, and schema updates.

Once configured, your data is automatically updated daily within BigQuery, providing your teams with instant access to comprehensive subscription operational and analytical data from Zuora Billing. This ready access empowers your teams to make prompt, informed decisions based on valuable insights.

## How the Zuora Connector for BigQuery works

The Zuora Connector for BigQuery establishes a direct connection between your Zuora account and your BigQuery environment. This link is created through a one-time setup process in which you enter your BigQuery project credentials. Once configured, the connector ensures the automated synchronization of your Zuora data into your designated BigQuery dataset daily.

This connector is specifically tailored to integrate seamlessly with BigQuery, enhancing your data analytics and reporting capabilities within your existing data infrastructure.
