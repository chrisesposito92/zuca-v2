# View your workflow access permissions

If you are not a Platform Administrator, follow the how-to in this topic to view your access permission for Workflow.

1.  Navigate to Administration \> Manage Users .
2.  On the Users page, locate and click your username.
3.  In Basic Information , locate Zuora Platform Role to view your role.

If you want to update your Workflows access permission, contact a Platform Administrator of your tenant.
