Enable your organization to access your Zuora data directly from your Delta Lake

## How Zuora Connector for Delta Lake works

Zuora Connector for Delta Lake establishes a seamless connection between your Zuora account and your Delta Lake destination. This is accomplished through a simple, one-time setup process where you input your Delta Lake credentials. Once configured, the connector enables automatic synchronization of your Zuora data to the specified Delta Lake destination on a daily basis. This connector is specifically tailored to integrate with Delta Lake, offering you flexibility and straightforward integration into your existing data infrastructure.
