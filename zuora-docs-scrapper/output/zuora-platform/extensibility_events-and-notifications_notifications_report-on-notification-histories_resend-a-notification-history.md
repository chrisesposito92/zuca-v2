# Resend a notification history

Learn how to resend a notification history on the Setup Profiles, Notifications and Email Templates page.

1.  Navigate to in the left navigation menu.
2.  Click the Email History or Callout History tab.
3.  Locate the notification history you want to resend from the list.
4.  Click Resend.
5.  In the confirmation dialog, click Resend.
