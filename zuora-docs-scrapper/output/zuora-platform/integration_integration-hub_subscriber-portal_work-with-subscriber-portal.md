# Work with Subscriber Portal

Learn how to invite users to your portal and monitor their activities effectively.

After you complete the configuration, you can invite users to your portal and monitor user activities.

-   [Invite Users to Portal](/zuora-platform/integration/integration-hub/subscriber-portal/work-with-subscriber-portal/invite-users-to-portal)

-   [View Activation Activities](/zuora-platform/integration/integration-hub/subscriber-portal/work-with-subscriber-portal/view-activation-activities)


## Monitor User Activities

After a user has received an activation email from the app or activated their account in the portal, you can monitor their activities.

## View Other Activities

If you want to view other activities of end users, enter the Ecommerce Portal Setup page and navigate to User Logs > Quick Stats . The real-time activity logs of all users are displayed. You can track their activities in the EVENT column.
