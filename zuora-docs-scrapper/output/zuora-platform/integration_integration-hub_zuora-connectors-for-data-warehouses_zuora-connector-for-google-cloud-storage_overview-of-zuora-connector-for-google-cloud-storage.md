# Overview of Zuora Connector for Google Cloud Storage

Introduction to Zuora Connector for Google Cloud Storage

Enable your organization to directly access your Zuora data through Google Cloud Storage. This integration involves a straightforward one-time setup process, which removes the typical technical challenges associated with managing data extracts, integrations, and schema updates. Once configured, your data is automatically updated daily within Google Cloud Storage, providing your teams with instant access to comprehensive subscription operational and analytical data from Zuora Billing.

This ready access empowers your teams to make prompt, informed decisions based on valuable insights.

Note:

The Zuora Connector for Data Warehouses feature is in the Early availability phase. This is a paid add-on. If you are interested in joining our early availability program, please reach out to your Zuora Representative for further details.

## How the Zuora Connector for Google Cloud Storage works

The Zuora Connector for Google Cloud Storage establishes a direct connection between your Zuora account and your Google Cloud Storage environment. This link is created through a one-time setup process in which you configure your Google Cloud credentials.

Once configured, the connector ensures the automated synchronization of your Zuora data into your designated Google Cloud Storage bucket daily. This connector is specifically tailored to integrate seamlessly with Google Cloud Storage, enhancing your data storage and analytics capabilities within your existing Google Cloud infrastructure.
