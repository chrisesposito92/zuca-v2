# Contractual Consumption Metrics

Monitor your contract's progress against allowances to forecast overages and manage your budget effectively.

View your contract's progress against allowances to forecast upcoming overages and manage your budget with subscription creation.

-   Billing Volume: Shows your current invoice volume (transaction volume) versus the contractual allowance. This section also reflects OLI + subscription usage metrics. For example, the committed volume is 165 M, and the billing usage volume is 45 M. A snapshot of the committed volume is taken at each month's end.

-   Revenue Volume: Shows the revenue transaction volume versus the contractual allowance.

-   Active Subscribers - Billing: Displays the number of active subscribers; a snapshot is taken at the end of each month.

-   Dates: Dates indicate the contract term or overage term.
