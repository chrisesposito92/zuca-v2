# Sync Account

Learn how to sync your Salesforce CPQ account with Zuora by navigating to the account, accessing the dropdown menu, and selecting the Send to Zuora button.

1.  Navigate to the Salesforce CPQ account.
2.  Click the dropdown menu.
3.  Select the Send to Zuora button created during connector Setup.
