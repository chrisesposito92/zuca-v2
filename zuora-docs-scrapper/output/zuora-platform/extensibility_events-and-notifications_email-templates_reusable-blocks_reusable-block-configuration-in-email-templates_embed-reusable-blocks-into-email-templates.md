# Embed reusable blocks into email templates

Learn how to embed reusable blocks into email templates.

1.  Navigate to in the left navigation menu.
2.  Click the Email Templates tab.
3.  In the Email Templates sub-tab, locate the email template you want to embed the reusable block into and click Edit in the last column. The Edit Email Template dialog opens.
4.  In the Email Body section, click Blocks in the email template editor.

5.  Add reusable blocks:
    1.  (Optional) Click a category or enter a tag name in the Search field to filter related reusable blocks.
    2.  Drag a reusable block to the desired location in the email body.
    3.  Repeat steps a and b to add additional reusable blocks as needed.
6.  Click Save.
