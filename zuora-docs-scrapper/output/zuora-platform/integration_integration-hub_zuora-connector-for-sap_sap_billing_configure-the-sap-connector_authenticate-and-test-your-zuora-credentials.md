# Authenticate and test your Zuora credentials

Learn how to authenticate your Zuora and connector environments by navigating to the Integration Hub, selecting SAP GL, entering authentication details, and validating the connection.

1.  Navigate to Marketplace > Integration Hub .
2.  Search and select SAP GL (Billing) .
3.  Click Settings .
4.  Enter the Client ID and Client Secret for the Zuora client in the Zuora Authentication section. For more information on how to generate Client ID and Client Secret, see [Create an OAuth Client for a User](/zuora-platform/system-management/administrator-settings/manage-users).
5.  Click Test Connection to validate your authentication details.
6.  Click Save .
7.  To configure SAP Connector:
    1.  Click Settings.
    2.  Enter the Host URL, Username, Password, Role Name, and Client Name.
    3.  Click Test Connection to validate your authentication details.
    4.  Click Save.
