# Enable history tracking for the account field

Learn how to enable history tracking for the Account field of the Billing Account object to support bulk changes to CRM Account IDs.

Enable History Tracking for the Account field of the Billing Account object (`Zuora__CustomerAccount__c.Account__c`). This setting supports the ability to change CRM Account IDs in bulk.

To enable History Tracking for the Account field of the Billing Account object:

1.  Navigate to .
2.  Click Billing Account .
3.  In the Custom Fields and Relationships section, click Set History Tracking .
4.  On the Billing Account Field History page, select Account .
5.  Click Save .
