# Salesforce CPQ connector sync

The Salesforce CPQ and Zuora integration streamlines sales and billing processes by synchronizing accounts, orders, and products, enhancing operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Salesforce CPQ and Zuora integration facilitate seamless communication and data management. It enhances operational efficiency by accurately handling account and order information. Key functionalities include account and order synchronization, support for usage-based orders, and additional features like syncing of Invoice Owner and Subscription Owner. You can streamline sales and billing processes with this integration, enhancing customer satisfaction.
