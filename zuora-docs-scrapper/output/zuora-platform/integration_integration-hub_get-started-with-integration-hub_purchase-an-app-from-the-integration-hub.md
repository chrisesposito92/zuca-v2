# Purchase an app from the Integration Hub

Learn how to purchase apps in the Marketplace that is available in your tenant.

1.  Within Zuora, navigate to .

    Available apps are listed.

2.  Find the app that you want to purchase, and click Purchase on the overview page of the app.

    Zuora will contact you with information about completing the purchase and setting up the app.

3.  To view your purchased apps, navigate to .

Purchased apps can be used by all Zuora administrators and Zuora standard users in your organization.

You must install an app before you can use it.
