Access Zuora data directly in Generic Postgres with daily refresh for seamless, insight-driven decisions

## How the Zuora Connector for Generic Postgres works

The Zuora Connector for Generic Postgres facilitates a direct connection between your Zuora account and your Generic Postgres environment. This connection is established through a one-time setup process during which you configure your Generic Postgres credentials.

Once configured, the connector automates the synchronization of your Zuora data into your specified Generic Postgres environment on a daily basis. Tailored to seamlessly integrate with Generic Postgres, this connector enhances your data storage and analytics capabilities within your infrastructure.
