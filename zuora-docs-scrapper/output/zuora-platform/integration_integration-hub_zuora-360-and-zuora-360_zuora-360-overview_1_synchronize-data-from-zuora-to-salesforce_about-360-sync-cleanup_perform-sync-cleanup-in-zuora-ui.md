# Perform sync cleanup in Zuora UI

Learn to perform 360 Sync Cleanup in Zuora UI that removes Zuora data from Salesforce.

1.  In the Zuora application, navigate to Settings > Commerce and click Synchronize Salesforce.com Data .
2.  In the Manual Sync section on the Zuora for Salesforce - 360 Sync page, click the Sync arrow and select what you want to clean up.
3.  Click clean up , and click yes in the confirmation window.
