# Configure email notifications in Revenue connector for NetSuite

Learn how to configure notifications for success and warning messages.

Email alerts are preconfigured to be sent for connector failures. You can also configure email notifications to be sent for success and warning scenarios.

1.  From the connector, click the wheel icon.
2.  Configure email notifications as you require.
3.  Add email recipients in the Email Configuration window and save.
