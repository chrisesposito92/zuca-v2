# Zuora Connector for Salesforce CRM

The Zuora Connector for Salesforce CRM enables seamless synchronization of customer, subscription, and billing data between Zuora and Salesforce. Designed to streamline revenue operations, the connector ensures that CRM teams always have accurate, real-time billing insights within Salesforce.
