# Configure accounting codes, accounting and GL segmentation rules

Learn how to configure accounting codes, accounting rules, and GL segmentation rules at the parent organization level, with automatic inheritance by child organizations.

Accounting rules, accounting codes, and GL segmentation rules can be set up at the parent organization level and the settings will be automatically inherited by the child organizations.

## To configure accounting codes for multiple organizations

For steps to configure accounting codes for your parent/root organization see [Configure accounting codes](/accounts-receivable/finance/zuora-finance-settings/configure-accounting-codes).

## To configure accounting rules for multiple organizations

For steps to configure accounting rules for your parent/root organization , see [Configure Accounting Rules](/accounts-receivable/finance/zuora-finance-settings/configure-accounting-rules).

## To configure GL segmentation rules for multiple organizations

For steps to configure GL segmentation rules for your parent/root organization , see [Configure GL Segmentation Rules](/accounts-receivable/finance/zuora-finance-settings/gl-segmentation-rules/gl-segmentation-rules-configuration).
