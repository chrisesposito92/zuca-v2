# Overview of Zuora Connector for Azure Blob Storage

Access Zuora data directly through Azure Blob Storage

Note:

The Zuora Connector for Data Warehouses feature is in the Early availability phase. This is a paid add-on. If you are interested in joining our early availability program, please reach out to your Zuora Representative for further details.

Enable your organization to access Zuora data directly through Azure Blob Storage. This integration simplifies the setup process, eliminating common technical hurdles related to data extracts, integrations, and schema updates.

Once configured, your data is automatically refreshed daily within Azure Blob Storage, granting your teams immediate access to extensive subscription operational and analytical data from Zuora Billing. This seamless access enables your teams to swiftly make informed decisions based on valuable insights.

## How the Zuora Connector for Azure Blob Storage works

The Zuora Connector for Azure Blob Storage facilitates a direct connection between your Zuora account and your Azure Blob Storage environment. This connection is established through a one-time setup process during which you configure your Azure credentials.

Once configured, the connector automates the synchronization of your Zuora data into your specified Azure Blob Storage environment on a daily basis. Tailored to seamlessly integrate with Azure Blob Storage, this connector enhances your data storage and analytics capabilities within your Azure infrastructure.
