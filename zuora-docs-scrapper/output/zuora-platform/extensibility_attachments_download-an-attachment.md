# Download an attachment

Learn how to download an attachment associated with a custom account, subscription, invoice, credit memo, or debit memo object.

1.  Open the detail page of the target object, scroll down to the Attachments section.
2.  Find the target attachment by the title.
3.  Click the action menu bar and click Download.
