# Consumption Insights

Consumption Insights is a machine learning-powered feature that optimizes revenue through deep analytics on usage-based metrics, supporting users of usage-based pricing models.

Consumption Insights is a machine learning-powered feature designed to help your business optimize revenue by providing deep analytics on usage-based metrics. This feature supports usage-based pricing model users. By leveraging data-driven insights, you can accurately forecast revenue, identify top products and customers, and mitigate churn risk.

## Key Capabilities of Consumption Insights

Overview Dashboard

-   Provides a comprehensive snapshot of total billed usage trends.
-   Includes historical data and forecasting and anomaly detection for the current and upcoming months.
-   Identifies top 5 products and top 5 customers based on billed usage amounts.

Account Insights

-   Displays detailed usage trends for each customer account.

-   Opportunity Scoring ranks customers based on increasing usage patterns, highlighting up-sell or cross-sell potential.

-   Risk Scoring identifies accounts with declining usage, indicating potential churn risks.


Billed Amount Drivers

-   Analyzes billed amounts by product or customer to uncover key revenue drivers.
-   Provides insights into high-performing products and customers.

Forecasting & Anomaly Detection

-   Predicts future revenue and usage trends using historical time series data.
-   Identifies anomalies that may require business intervention.
-   Enables proactive decision-making to maximize revenue opportunities.
