# Assigning business process security policy permissions

Learn how to assign security policy permissions for business processes in Workday.

1.  Log in and access the Workday console using your administrator credentials.
2.  Type "Edit Business Process Security Policy" and press Enter .
3.  Type "Accounting Journal Event" in the Business Process Type field and press Enter .
4.  In the Initiating Action section of Import Accounting Journal (WS Background Process), type "ISSG\_Zuora" in the Security Groups field and press Enter .
