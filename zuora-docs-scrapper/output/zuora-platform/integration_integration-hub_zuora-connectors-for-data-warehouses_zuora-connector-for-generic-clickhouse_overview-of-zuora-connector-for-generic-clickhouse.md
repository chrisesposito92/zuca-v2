# Overview of Zuora Connector for Generic ClickHouse

Access Zuora data directly from Zuora Connector for Generic ClickHouse

Zuora Connector for Generic ClickHouse establishes a seamless connection between your Zuora account and your ClickHouse destination. This is accomplished through a simple, one-time setup process where you input your Generic ClickHouse credentials. Once configured, the connector enables automatic synchronization of your Zuora data to the specified ClickHouse destination on a daily basis. This connector is specifically tailored to integrate with Generic ClickHouse, offering you flexibility and straightforward integration into your existing data infrastructure.

Enable your organization to access Zuora data directly from Zuora Connector for Generic ClickHouse. This integration consists of a simple, one-step setup process, eliminating the need for conventional engineering efforts that are required to manage data extracts, integrations, and data schema changes. Once this sync is set up, your data will be automatically updated every 24 hours, providing your teams with instant access to Zuora Billing's comprehensive subscription, operational and analytical data. This access will allow your teams to make timely, data-driven decisions based on valuable insights.

Note:

The Zuora Connector for Data Warehouses feature is in the Early Adopter phase. This is a paid add-on. If you are interested in joining our early adopter program, please reach out to your Zuora Representative for further details.

## Prerequisites for Zuora Connector for Generic ClickHouse

To begin the setup process for the Zuora Connector for ClickHouse, ensure you have the following prerequisites:

-   Zuora Tenant ID: This is your unique identifier within Zuora. To find your Tenant ID, see [Managing your tenant profile](/zuora-platform/system-management/administrator-settings/manage-your-tenant-profile).

-   ClickHouse Database: Ensure that your ClickHouse database is pre-configured and ready for connection. Make sure you have the necessary permissions to manage and access the database.

-   ClickHouse Database Credentials: You will need the username and password for your ClickHouse database to establish the connection.


Once you have the prerequisites, you can proceed with the configuration to establish the connection between your Zuora account and your ClickHouse database. This enables streamlined data synchronization and enhances your data storage and analytics capabilities.
