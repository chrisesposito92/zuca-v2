# Revenue Analytics dashboard

Zuora Revenue is capable of ingesting booking data and performing continuous month close and real-time data validation and reconciliations. With these capabilities, the Revenue Analysis dashboard is introduced to provide real-time insight into revenue to the company's management team. These real-time revenue statistics and insights can help the management team make critical and strategic decisions.

This dashboard contains the following tabs to provide you with a persona-driven experience to view and analyze your revenue in a multifaceted way.

-   Overview
-   Revenue Insights

-   Top Bookings

-   Forecast

-   History


For more details about each tab, see [Getting started with the analytics dashboard](/zuora-revenue/month-end-process/revenue-analytics-dashboard) .

## Availability

The dashboard is available under the following conditions:

-   This feature is in Limited Availability . We are actively soliciting feedback from a small set of early adopters.

-   To enable this feature for your tenant, you must submit a request at [Zuora Global Support](https://support.zuora.com/hc/en-us) . The support team checks your eligibility to be part of this program and lets you know.

-   The minimum patch version to have this feature enabled is 37.008.03.00. To upgrade your instance to the latest version, contact [Zuora Global Support](https://support.zuora.com/hc/en-us) .

-   If you have any further questions or feedback, please contact your Zuora account representative to discuss.


## Prerequisites

To access this dashboard, make sure the following requirements are met:

-   Zuora unified authentication (also known as Zuora SSO) is enabled for the Zuora Revenue tenant.

-   The current user role has appropriate access to this dashboard. The role privileges are usually configured by the system administrator on the Setups > Security > Roles page.
