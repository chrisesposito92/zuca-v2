# OneID settings

This article provides an overview of OneID settings and guides organization admins through the configuration process.

This article introduces OneID settings to you and provides links to topics that describe the configuration steps in OneID. You must be an organization admin to configure these settings.

## Open the Settings page

To open the Settings page in OneID, click your avatar in the upper right of the OneID portal and then click Settings .
