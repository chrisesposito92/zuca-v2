# Zuora Revenue Testing Environments

Zuora Revenue offers various testing environments to evaluate the application, APIs, and integrations, helping you choose the right setup for your needs.

Zuora Revenue provides different types of testing environments for you to test the Zuora Revenue application, APIs, and supported integration

Testing environments represent private tenant access to the Zuora Revenue application, APIs, and supported integration. This article describes the different types of Zuora Revenue testing environments and is targeted to help you determine the appropriate environment for your needs.

Zuora offers the following testing environments for the Zuora Revenue application.

-   [Production copy environment (Sandbox)](/basics/environments/zuora-revenue-testing-environments)Intended for release preview, system testing, and data modeling. It comes with a copy of your production data. Data and the application are refreshed on demand. By default, the customer gets 1 or 2 tenants as per contract. Zuora customers can purchase any number of additional tenants in this environment for a recurring annual fee.
-   [Migration environment](/basics/environments/zuora-revenue-testing-environments)Intended for heavy performance testing, load testing, and data conversion or migration. It comes with a copy of your production data if applicable. Zuora customers can purchase high-performance migration tenants on a monthly basis.
