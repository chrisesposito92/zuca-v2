# Transfer accounting

Learn about the process of transferring unposted journal entries from Zuora Revenue to your company's general ledger.

Transfer accounting is the process of transferring unposted journal entries (either in a detailed or summarized format) from Zuora Revenue as a revenue sub-ledger to your company's general ledger.
