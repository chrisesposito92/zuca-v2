# Introduction to standard field mapping

Explore predefined field mappings in Zuora Revenue transaction upload templates to transform synchronized data from Zuora Billing into recognizable fields.

Billing - Revenue Integration offers a spectrum of predefined field mappings in Zuora Revenue transaction upload templates. With these field mappings, Zuora Revenue can transform the synchronized data from Zuora Billing into the fields that Zuora Revenue can recognize.

To correctly ingest and map relevant subscription data from Zuora Billing, Zuora Revenue introduces a set of subscription and amendment Staging fields. As such, Zuora Revenue is now "subscription aware".

For the detailed field mappings between Zuora Billing and Zuora Revenue, see the following topics for more information:

-   Sales order data mapping.

-   [Invoice item data mapping](/zuora-revenue/advanced-revenue-operations/mapping-between-zuora-billing-and-zuora-revenue/invoice-item-data-mapping).

-   Invoice item adjustment data mapping.

-   Credit memo item data mapping.

-   Debit memo item data mapping.
