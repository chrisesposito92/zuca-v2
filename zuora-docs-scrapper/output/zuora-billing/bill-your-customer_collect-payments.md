# Collect payments

You can use Zuora Payments to efficiently collect payments from your customers after billing documents are prepared.

After billing documents are ready for your customers, you need a payment solution to turn invoices into cash with varied preferences for pre-built payment gateways. Zuora provides an end-to-end solution called Zuora Payments for this purpose.
