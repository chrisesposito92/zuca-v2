# Manage subscriptions and orders in the Orders tenant

You can follow these steps to manage subscriptions and orders in the Orders tenant.

If you are an Orders tenant user, complete the following steps to view subscriptions or orders tied to a customer account.

1.  On the account details page, navigate to the Orders & Subscriptions section.
2.  Click one of the following tabs. Each tab displays subscriptions or orders in descending order of the last modified dates.

    -   Subscriptions-Owned - You can sort the subscriptions by clicking Sort at the bottom of this section. In a new window that opens, click the up or down arrow above subscription numbers, and then click close to apply the change.
    -   Subscriptions-Invoiced
    -   Orders

3.  (Optional) If you want to view all subscriptions or orders tied to the customer account, click View All at the bottom of the tab to view them on a new page.
4.  In the tab or the new page, click a subscription number or order number to view details.

    From the bottom of the Orders and Subscription section, you can create an order by clicking Create Orders in the Orderstab. For more information, see Orders Tutorials.
