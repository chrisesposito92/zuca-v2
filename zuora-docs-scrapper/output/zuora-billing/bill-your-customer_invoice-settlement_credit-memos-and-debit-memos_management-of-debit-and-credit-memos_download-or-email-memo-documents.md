# Download or email memo documents

Learn how to download or email memo documents

After credit and debit memos have been generated, you can either download or email the memo PDF documents. To get the latest version of the memo documents, you need to regenerate the memo PDFs.

Note:

Up to 20,000 line items are allowed in a single memo PDF file.
