# Upload billing document templates

Learn how to upload billing document templates

You can upload your custom billing document (invoice, credit memo, and debit memo) template to Zuora.

To upload a billing document template, complete the following steps.

1.  Click your username at the top right corner and click Billing > Manage Billing Document Configuration.
2.  In the Manage Invoice, Credit/Debit Memo Templates tab, click Add new template.
3.  In the Name field, specify a name for the new template.
4.  From the Template Type list, select a template type.
5.  Click Choose File to select your template file.
6.  (Optional): Select the Do not display zero value invoice line items check box.
7.  Click save.
