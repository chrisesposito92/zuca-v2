# Download an attachment

This topic explains how to download an attachment of a Customer Account, Subscription, Invoice, Credit Memo, or Debit Memo detail page.

To download an attachment:

1.  On the detail page of the target object, scroll down to the Attachments section.
2.  Find the target attachment you want to download by the title.
3.  Click the action menu bar and click Download.
