# Country-specific configurations management

This topic outlines the management of country-specific configurations for various service providers, including Sovos and Avalara.

Except common e-invoicing configurations including configuring service providers, business regions, and account e-invoicing profiles, each country also has to conduct country-specific configurations.

The following country-specific configurations are classified for different service providers. These countries are countries Zuora pre-integrated for corresponding service providers.
