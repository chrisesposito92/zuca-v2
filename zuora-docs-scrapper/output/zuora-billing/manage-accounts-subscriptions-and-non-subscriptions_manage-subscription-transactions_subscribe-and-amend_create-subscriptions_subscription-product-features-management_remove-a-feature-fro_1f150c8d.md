# Remove a Feature from a Product in an Amendment

This article explains how to remove a feature from a product in an amendment.

To remove a feature from a product in an amendment :

1.  Create an amendment of the type, New Product or Update a Product
2.  In the Amendment Details section, click Del for the feature you want to remove.
3.  Click OK to confirm.
