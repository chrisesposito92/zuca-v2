# Manage memo templates

Learn how to edit or remove memo templates

You can edit or remove credit memo and debit memo templates in Zuora Billing setting.

## Edit memo templates

To edit a credit memo or debit memo template:

1.  Click your username at the top right corner and click Billing > Manage Billing Document Configuration.
2.  In the Manage Invoice, Credit/Debit Memo Templates tab, click Edit.
3.  Edit the template name or upload the updated template file.
4.  Click Save.

## Remove memo templates

To remove a credit memo or debit memo template:

1.  Click your username at the top right corner and click Billing > Manage Billing Document Configuration.
2.  In the Manage Invoice, Credit/Debit Memo Templates tab, click Remove.

Note that you cannot remove the memo default template.
