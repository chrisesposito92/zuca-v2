# Adopt orders after enabling Orders Harmonization

This task topic explains how to adopt Orders by enabling the Orders UI and managing subscriptions with Orders Harmonization.

When Orders Harmonization is enabled, the Orders UI remains turned off by default. You can switch to the Orders UI to create and manage the subscriptions, and access the other functionalities empowered by Orders.

1.  Click your avatar at the top right and navigate to Billing \> Define Default Subscription and Order Settings.
2.  Identify the Default Order Settings section and click Edit.
3.  Switch the Enable Order UI? setting to [Default Subscription and Order Settings](/zuora-billing/set-up-zuora-billing/billing-settings-configuration/subscription-and-order-settings/default-order-settings)Yes. For more information on the setting, refer to the section.
