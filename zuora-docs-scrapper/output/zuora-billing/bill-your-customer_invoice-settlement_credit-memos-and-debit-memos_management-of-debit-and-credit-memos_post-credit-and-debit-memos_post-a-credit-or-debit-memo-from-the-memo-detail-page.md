# Post a credit or debit memo from the memo detail page

Post a credit or debit memo from the memo detail page

To post a credit memo or debit memo from the memo detail page, complete these steps.

1.  Navigate to Billing > Credit and Debit Memos in the navigation section.
2.  On the Credit and Debit Memos page, click the memo number in the Credit Memos or Debit Memos tab. The memo detail page opens.
3.  Click Post. A dialog is displayed to confirm the action.
4.  Click Save to confirm the action.
