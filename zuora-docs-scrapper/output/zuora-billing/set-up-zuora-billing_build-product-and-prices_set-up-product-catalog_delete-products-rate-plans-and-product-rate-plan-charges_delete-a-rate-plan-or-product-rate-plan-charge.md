# Delete a rate plan or product rate plan charge

Learn how to delete a rate plan or product rate plan charge that is not linked to a subscription.

You can also remove a rate plan and product rate plan charge if the applicable items are not linked to a subscription.

To remove a rate plan or rate plan charge that is not linked to a subscription:

1.  Open the product detail page containing the rate plan or rate plan charge to delete.
2.  Locate the target rate plan or rate plan charge, and hover over it to find the Delete icon on the right side.
3.  Click the Delete icon to delete the rate plan or product rate plan charge.
4.  When prompted, click Yes to remove the rate plan or rate plan charge.

After the rate plan or product rate plan charge is removed, it will no longer be displayed in the UI.
