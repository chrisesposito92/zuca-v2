# Transactions in Zuora

Transactions in Zuora involve the exchange or transfer of money between you and your customer, impacting invoice or credit balances.

A transaction is an exchange or transfer of money between you and your customer (billing account). A transaction impacts the total invoice balance or credit balance, and can be transferred to an accounting system.

Note:

Invoice Adjustment is deprecated in Production environments. Zuora recommends using the [Invoice Item Adjustments](/zuora-billing/bill-your-customer/invoice-item-adjustments/overview-of-invoice-item-adjustments) or Invoice Settlement feature instead.

Invoices item adjustments exist only in relation to the invoice. These transactions cannot stand alone.

The credit balance adjustment represents increases and decreases in the credit balance. You can refund money to a customer from the payment or from the credit balance.
