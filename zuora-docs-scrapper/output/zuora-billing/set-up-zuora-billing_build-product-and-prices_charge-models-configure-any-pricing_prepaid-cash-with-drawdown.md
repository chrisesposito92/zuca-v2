# Prepaid cash with drawdown

The Prepaid Cash with Drawdown feature allows for payment collection and reservation on customer accounts, facilitating subscription payments.

The Prepaid Cash with Drawdown feature enables you to collect payment from your customers and then deposit the payment on their customer account as reserved payment. Subscriptions can be configured to consume this reserved payment amount upon invoice post or payment run. For more information, see Prepaid Cash with Drawdown .
