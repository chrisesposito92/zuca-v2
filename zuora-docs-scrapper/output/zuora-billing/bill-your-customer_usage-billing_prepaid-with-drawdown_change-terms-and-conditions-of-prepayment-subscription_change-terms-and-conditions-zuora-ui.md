# Change terms and conditions - Zuora UI

Learn how to change the terms and conditions of a subscription and add a product prepayment charge using the Zuora UI.

You can use the Zuora UI to add a product prepayment charge to a subscription.

1.  [Change the terms and conditions of a subscription](/zuora-billing/manage-accounts-subscriptions-and-non-subscriptions/manage-subscription-transactions/orders/order-actions-tutorials/terms-and-conditions-of-subscriptions/use-the-zuora-ui---change-terms-and-conditions).
2.  Select the product.
3.  Update the initial term details if needed. In the example, you can change the Initial Term to 9.
