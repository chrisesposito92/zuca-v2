# Payment solution

Zuora Payments provides a comprehensive solution for managing payment processes, including gateway integrations, payment methods, and automated collections.

Zuora Payments is an end-to-end payment solution. It offers a simple way to turn invoices into cash with varied preferences for pre-built payment gateways. It can also manage automated payment processes and optimize recurring collections with automated dunning and machine learning through a product called Zuora Collections. For more information, see the Zuora Payments section in the Knowledge Center. The following information is described.
