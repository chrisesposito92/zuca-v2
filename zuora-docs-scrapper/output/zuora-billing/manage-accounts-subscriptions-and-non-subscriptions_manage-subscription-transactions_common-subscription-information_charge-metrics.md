# Charge Metrics

Zuora Charge Metrics provides access to key metrics for Rate Plan Charges, including Gross MRR, Discount MRR, Net MRR, Gross TCV, Discount TCV, and Net TCV.

Zuora Charge Metrics provides a service for you to access the following key metrics for Rate Plan Charges in Zuora.

-   Gross MRR

-   Discount MRR

-   Net MRR

-   Gross TCV

-   Discount TCV

-   Net TCV


For the definition of MRR and how MRR is calculated in Zuora, see Monthly Recurring Revenue .

For the definition of TCV and how TCV is calculated in Zuora, see Total Contract Value .

Note:

Charge Metrics do not support and handle charges with a zero amount, that is, MRR = 0 or TCV = 0. When all charges in a subscription have a zero amount, Charge Metrics are not available for this subscription.
