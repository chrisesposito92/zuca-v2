# Manage subscriptions, orders, and amendments in the Orders Harmonization tenant

This task topic lists the steps in this page to manage subscriptions, orders, and amendments in the Orders Harmonization tenant.

If you are an Orders Harmonization tenant user, complete the following steps to view subscriptions, orders, or amendments tied to a customer account.

1.  On the account details page, navigate to the Orders & Subscriptions section.
2.  Click one of the following tabs. Each tab displays subscriptions, orders, or amendments in descending order of the last modified dates.

    -   Subscriptions-Owned (displayed by default) You can sort the subscriptions by clicking Sort at the bottom of this section. In a new window that opens, click the up or down arrow above subscription numbers, and then click close to apply the change.
    -   Subscriptions-Invoiced
    -   Orders
    -   Pending Amendments
    -   All Amendments

3.  (Optional) If you want to view all subscriptions or amendments tied to the customer account, click View All at the bottom of the tab to view the details of subscriptions or amendments on a new page.
4.  In the tab or the new page, click a subscription number, amendment number order number, or amendment number to view the subscription or amendment details. From the bottom of the Orders & Subscriptions section, you can create a subscription or order.

    -   Click Create New Subscription in the Subscriptions-Ownedtab.
    -   Click Create Order in the Orders tab.

    For more information, see Orders Tutorials.
