# Delete a Customer Account

This task topic explains how to delete a customer account, including all associated information, such as subscriptions and invoices.

1.  Navigate to Customers > Customer Accounts in the left-hand navigation to view the existing accounts.
2.  Locate the target account and click the Delete icon on the right side of the row.
3.  Click Delete to confirm the action.

Deleting an account deletes all account information, including subscriptions, invoices, payments, and adjustments.
