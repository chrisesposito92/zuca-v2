# Manage subscriptions and orders in the Orders tenant

This task topic lists the steps to manage subscriptions and orders in the Orders tenant.

If you are an Orders tenant user, complete the following steps to view subscriptions, orders, and dynamic usage charges tied to a customer account.

1.  On the account details page, navigate to the Orders & Subscriptions & Dynamic Usage Charges section.
2.  Click one of the following tabs. Each tab displays the records in descending order of the last modified dates.

    -   Subscriptions-Owned
        Note: You can sort the subscriptions by clicking Sort at the bottom of this section. In a new window that opens, click the up or down arrow above subscription numbers, and then click the Close button at the bottom of the window to apply the change.

    -   Subscriptions-Invoiced
    -   Orders
    -   Dynamic Usage Charges

3.  (Optional) If you want to view all records tied to the customer account, click View All at the bottom of the tab to view them on a new page.
4.  In the tab or the new page, click a subscription number or order number to view the subscription or order details.

    To create an order, click Create Order under the Orders tab.
