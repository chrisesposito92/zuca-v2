# Update products on subscriptions with future-dated removals using the Zuora UI

This article explains how to add a product to a subscription.

You can use the Zuora UI to update a product even when a future-dated Remove Product amendment already exist on the subscription.

Select the Show removed products check box before selecting a product to update in the Amendment Detail panel. The products with Remove Product amendments are shown in the select product list. The following figure shows a rate plan that has a Remove Product amendment on the subscription.
