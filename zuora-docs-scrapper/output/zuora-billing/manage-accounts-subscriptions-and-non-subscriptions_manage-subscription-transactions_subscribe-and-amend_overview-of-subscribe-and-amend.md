# Overview of Subscribe and Amend

This topic explains about the Subscribe and Amend function, which enables customers to manage their product or service subscriptions and make amendments as needed.

Subscribe and Amend is the existing function that allows your customers to subscribe to your products or services through subscriptions, and modify the subscriptions through amendments.

If you are in the Subscribe and Amend tenant, you can perform the following tasks:

-   Create subscriptions
-   Amend subscriptions
