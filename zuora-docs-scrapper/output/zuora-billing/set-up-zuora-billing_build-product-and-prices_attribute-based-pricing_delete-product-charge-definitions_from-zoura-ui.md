# From Zoura UI

Learn how to navigate the Zoura UI to manage and delete charge definitions.

1.  Navigate to .
2.  Find the charge that contains the target charge definition and enter the edit charge mode.
3.  At the bottom of the Charge Amount section, click Manage Charge Definitions . The Manage Charge Definitions page opens.
4.  In the Charge Definition Mapping List section, locate the charge definition and click the Delete icon on the right side.
5.  In the pop-up window, click Delete to confirm the operation.
