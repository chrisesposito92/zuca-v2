# View order line item for a customer account

This topic explains how to view order line items for a customer account in the Zuora application.

If a customer account is charged for order line items, you can get the order line item information from the Customer Account page.

1.  Log in to the Zuora application and then navigate to Customers > Customer Accounts . All customer accounts are listed on the page.
2.  Search for the target customer account to open the customer account details page. For information about how to search for an account, see Find Customer Accounts .
3.  In the Orders & Subscriptions section, click the Orders tab. The order line items that are associated with the current account are listed in the Order Line Items column.
