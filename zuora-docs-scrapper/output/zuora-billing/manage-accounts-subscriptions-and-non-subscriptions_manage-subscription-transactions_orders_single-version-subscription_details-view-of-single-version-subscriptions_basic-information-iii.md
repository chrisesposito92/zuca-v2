# Basic Information III

This topic provides detailed information by clicking "More details" in the Basic Information section, and explore tabs for Additional Information and Custom Fields.

You can click More details in this Basic Information section to open a panel at the right, and switch between the Additional Information and Custom Fields tabs on the panel to view more information.

Additional information includes Billing & payment, Scheduled Actions, and Quote information.

Billing & payment information includes:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Invoice Separately | Displays whether to create independent invoices per subscription . |
| Notes | Displays commentary for the subscription. |
