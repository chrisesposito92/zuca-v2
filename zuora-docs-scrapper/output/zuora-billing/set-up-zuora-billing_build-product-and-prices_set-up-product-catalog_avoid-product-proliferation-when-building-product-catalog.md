# Avoid product proliferation when building product catalog

Learn how to prevent unnecessary product proliferation in your catalog by understanding key elements like products, rate plans, and charges.

Your business only offers a few products to its subscribers, yet you're considering building a product catalog filled with SKUs. What's going wrong? How can you avoid a proliferation of products in your product catalog that becomes unwieldy over time?

To avoid unnecessary product proliferation, you must ensure you fully understand the key elements of a product catalog, such as products, rate plans, and charges, as well as understand when to use each.
