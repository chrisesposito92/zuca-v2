# Get started with Billing Schedule

Understand how to create, view, edit, and delete invoice schedules and items, and explore common use cases for the Billing Schedule feature.

You can try some operations to quickly get familiar with the Billing Schedule feature. For detailed instructions on each task, click the links below to go to the corresponding page.

To learn more about the basic concepts of this feature, see Overview of Billing Schedule .

After you have got appropriate user permissions, perform the following steps to create, view, edit, and delete invoice schedules and invoice schedule items:

The following operations are available at the invoice schedule level:

-   Create invoice schedules

-   Edit invoice schedules

-   Execute invoice schedules

-   Create bill runs for invoice schedules

-   View details of invoice schedules

-   Delete invoice schedules


To get a deeper understanding of the Billing feature, learn more about the common use cases of this feature:

-   Create single-year invoice schedules on new subscriptions with the same term

-   Cancel draft invoices generated in invoice schedules

-   Create single-year invoice schedules for charges with different start dates and end dates within one term

-   Create multiyear invoice schedules to bill charges within the same year

-   Create multiyear invoice schedules to bill second-year charges in the first year

-   Create single-year invoice schedules on new subscriptions with the same odd term

-   Remove charges from fully processed invoice schedules

-   Remove charges from partially processed invoice schedules

-   Manually review subscriptions through Subscription Renewal and create another invoice schedule

-   Update invoice schedules by adding subscription charges

-   Update invoice schedules by changing charge prices

-   Update dates and amounts of partially processed invoice schedules

-   Transfer owners of subscriptions associated with invoice schedules

-   Delete partially processed invoice schedules

-   Use custom and standard billing schedules for multiyear contracts
