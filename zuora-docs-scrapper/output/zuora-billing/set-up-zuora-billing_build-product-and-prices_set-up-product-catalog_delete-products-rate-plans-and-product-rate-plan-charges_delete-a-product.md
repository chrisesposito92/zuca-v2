# Delete a product

Learn how to delete a product that is not linked to a subscription from the product catalog.

To delete a product that is not linked to a subscription:

1.  Navigate to in the left-hand navigation section.
2.  Locate the product you want to delete.
3.  On the target product row, click the Delete icon.
4.  When prompted, click Delete to delete the product.

After the product is removed, it will no longer be displayed in the UI.
