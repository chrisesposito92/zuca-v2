# Delete credit and debit memos

Learn how to delete credit and debit memos

You can delete credit and debit memos only if you have the user permission and the memos are in Cancelled Status. See Billing Roles for more information about Billing user permissions.

To delete a credit memo or debit memo from the REST API, see [DELETE Credit Memo](https://www.zuora.com/developer/api-references/api/operation/DELETE_CreditMemo) or [DELETE Debit Memo](https://www.zuora.com/developer/api-references/api/operation/DELETE_DebitMemo).

To cancel a credit memo or debit memo from the Zuora UI, complete the following steps.

1.  Navigate to Billing > Credit and Debit Memos in the left-hand navigation section.
2.  On the Credit and Debit Memos page, click the Credit Memo or Debit Memo tab.
3.  Click the memo number that you want to delete.
4.  On the memo detail page, click Delete.
5.  Click Yes.
