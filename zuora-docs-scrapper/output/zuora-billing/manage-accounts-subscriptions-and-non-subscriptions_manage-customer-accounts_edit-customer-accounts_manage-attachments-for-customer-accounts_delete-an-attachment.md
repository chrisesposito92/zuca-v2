# Delete an attachment

This topic explains how to delete an attachment from a Customer Account, Subscription, Invoice, Credit Memo, or Debit Memo detail page.

To delete an attachment:

1.  On the detail page of the target object, scroll down to the Attachments section.
2.  Locate the target attachment you want to delete by the title.
3.  Click the action menu bar and click Delete.
4.  Click OK to confirm the deletion.
