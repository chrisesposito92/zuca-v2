# Basic Information I

The Basic Information section provides details about subscription, billing, payment, and custom fields, including a preview of the next invoice and quota information.

The Basic Information section includes information about the subscription, the preview of the next invoice, additional information about billing and payment as well as the quota, and custom field information.

The subscription information includes:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Customer Account | Click the customer account name to view more details about the subscription owner account. |
| Invoice Owner | Click the invoice owner name of the subscription to view more details about the invoice owner account. |
| Subscription Period | Displays the subscription period indicated through the subscription start and end dates. |
| Current Term | Displays the current term indicated through the term start and end dates.Auto renew on or off indicates whether the subscription is automatically renewed after the current term. |
| Net MRR | Displays net Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR). For MRR types, see Monthly Recurring Revenue .Click the Mrr Trending link to monitor the subscription lifecycle and MRR trending . |
| Net TCV | Displays net Total Contract Value (TCV), see Order Metrics . |
