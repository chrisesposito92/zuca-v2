# Deleting Subscriptions

This topic explains how to navigate customer accounts to delete a subscription.

To delete a subscription:

1.  Navigate to Customers > Customer Accounts in the left-hand navigation section.
2.  Select the customer account with the subscription that you want to delete.
3.  In the Customer Account detail screen, Scroll to the Subscriptions and Amendments area and click the Subscription Number of the subscription that you want to delete.
4.  Click Delete , then click OK to confirm the action.
