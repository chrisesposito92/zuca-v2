# Overview of Flexible Billing

This section provides an introduction to the Flexible Billing functionality, enabling efficient management of billing, taxation, and payments with features like Flexible Billing Attributes, and Billing Schedule.

This section introduces the Flexible Billing functionality and the features you can use to efficiently and flexibly manage billing, taxation, and payments at a more granular level other than the account level.

The Flexible Billing functionality comprises the Flexible Billing Attributes , Billing Schedule , Multiple Currencies , and Invoice Grouping features. All the features are generally available. You can enable the features in Sandbox and Production environments with the self-serve interface. For more information, see Enable billing features by yourself.
