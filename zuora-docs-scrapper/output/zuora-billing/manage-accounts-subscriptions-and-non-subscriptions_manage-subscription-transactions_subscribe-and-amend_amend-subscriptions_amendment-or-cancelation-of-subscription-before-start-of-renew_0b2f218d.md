# Steps to amend or cancel a subscription

This topic explains how to amend or cancel a subscription before the renewal term starts.

To amend or cancel a subscription before the start date of a renewal term that has been invoiced, complete the following steps:

1.  Create an amendment or cancel the subscription.
2.  Create a bill run.
3.  Post the new invoice.
