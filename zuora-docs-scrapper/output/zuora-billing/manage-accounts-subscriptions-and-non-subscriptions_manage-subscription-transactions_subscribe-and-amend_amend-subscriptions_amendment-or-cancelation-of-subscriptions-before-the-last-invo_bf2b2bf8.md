# Create or cancel a subscription before last invoice date

This topic explains how to amend or cancel a subscription before the last invoice date by creating an amendment, running a bill, and posting a new invoice.

To amend or cancel a subscription before the last invoice date, you:

1.  Create an amendment or cancel the subscription.
2.  Create a bill run.
3.  Post the new invoice.
