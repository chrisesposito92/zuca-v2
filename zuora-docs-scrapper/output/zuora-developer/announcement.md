#

Announcing the Redesigned Zuora Developer Center: New Features and a Fresh Look

Hello Developers!

We're excited to share that the Zuora Developer Center is getting a makeover. We've listened to your feedback and made some significant changes to improve your experience. While there's more to come, we want to introduce you to what's new and tell you about our plans for the future.

**Unified Top Navigation Bar**

Navigating through our site should be easy and intuitive. That's why we've introduced a new unified top navigation bar, allowing you to quickly access key areas within the developer center. This update will help you find what you need without getting lost in the process.

**Simplified Left-Hand Navigation**

Along with the new top navigation, we've also redesigned the left-hand navigation menu. It's now more straightforward and easier to follow, with a clear layout that helps you jump to different sections with minimal effort.

**Improved Tutorials and Guides**

To help you along your journey, we've simplified our tutorials and guides. These updated resources are designed to give you the essential information you need in a more accessible format. Whether you're just getting started or looking for advanced tips, our new tutorials and guides aim to support your work.

**Looking to the Future**

This is just the beginning. We're committed to creating even better content and adding more features to enhance your experience. While we continue to make improvements, your feedback will be invaluable in shaping the future of the Zuora Developer Center.

Thank you for being part of our community. We hope you enjoy the new updates, and we're excited to see how they help you on your developer journey.

Happy coding!

The Zuora Developer Team
